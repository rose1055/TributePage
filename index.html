Mr. Krabs

Mr. Krabs character was inspired by Stephan Hillenburg former manager. Stephen worked at a seafood restaurant where he met the manager. He was red headed and a former army cook. He also had a strong Maine accent that reminded him of a pirate.

Patrick

Hillenburg originally created Patrick to be a starfish character who was an angry and mean bar owner. He later developed his character to be similar to real starfishes, seemingly slow and dimwitted but also active and aggressive at times.

Fungas

Researchers named a fungus after Spongebob Squarepants. In 2011, American mycologists discovered a sponge-like substance in Lambir Hills National Park in Malaysia. The fungus was then named Spongiforma Spuarepantsii.

Squidward

Squidward is an octopus not a squid. Squidward is a 6 legged octopus instead of 8. The designers chose 6 instead of 8 limbs because it would be too much visually.
